Organic

Organic farming is a method of agriculture that emphasizes sustainability and natural methods. It involves avoiding the use of synthetic fertilizers and pesticides, and instead relying on natural methods such as crop rotation and composting..

Technology

Technological advancements have greatly impacted farming, allowing for more efficient and precise methods of planting, harvesting, and monitoring crops. Examples include the use of drones for crop monitoring and the development of precision farming tools for more accurate application of inputs.

Black Chia Seeds

Black chia seeds are small, black seeds that come from the plant Salvia hispanica. They have become increasingly popular in recent years due to their high nutritional content and potential health benefits. Black chia seeds are a good source of fiber, protein, and omega-3 fatty acids, and may help with weight loss, digestion, and heart health. They can be added to a variety of dishes, such as smoothies, oatmeal, and salads, or used as an egg substitute in baking.

White Chia Seeds

Chia is an annual herbaceous plant that can reach nearly 1 metre (3 feet) in height. Its lime-green leaves are oppositely arranged and have serrated (toothed) margins. The plant bears spikes of small blue, purple, or white flowers that have a high rate of self-pollination. The small oval seeds are about 1 mm (0.04 inch) in diametre and feature a shiny, mottled, or speckled seed coat that ranges in colour from dark brown to gray-white.
