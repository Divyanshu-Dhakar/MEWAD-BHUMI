About

Mewad Bhumi is a farming website that provides information and resources for farmers. Our goal is to support farmers in Mewad and beyond by providing the latest farming news, tips, and techniques. We also offer a marketplace for farmers to buy and sell their products, as well as a forum for farmers to connect and share their experiences. Whether you are a seasoned farmer or just getting started, Mewad Bhumi is here to help you succeed in your farming endeavors.

Black Chia Seed

Black chia seeds are small, black seeds that come from the plant Salvia hispanica. They have become increasingly popular in recent years due to their high nutritional content and potential health benefits. Black chia seeds are a good source of fiber, protein, and omega-3 fatty acids, and may help with weight loss, digestion, and heart health.

White Chia Seed

Chia is an annual herbaceous plant that can reach nearly 1 metre (3 feet) in height. Its lime-green leaves are oppositely arranged and have serrated (toothed) margins. The plant bears spikes of small blue, purple, or white flowers that have a high rate of self-pollination. The small oval seeds are about 1 mm (0.04 inch) in diametre and feature a shiny, mottled, or speckled seed coat that ranges in colour from dark brown to gray-white.
